Services we offer

Dee Strong Medical Delivery is committed to reliable and professional healthcare logistics:

Prescription Delivery

Timely delivery of prescribed medications from pharmacies directly to patients’ homes with full privacy and care.

Emergency Medical Runs

Fast dispatch of emergency supplies, equipment, or medication to clinics and hospitals within critical timelines.

Homecare Equipment Delivery

Delivery of wheelchairs, oxygen tanks, and other essential medical tools needed for at-home patient care.

Lab Sample Pickup

Secure transport of medical samples from patient locations to diagnostic labs, ensuring proper handling and speed.

Routine Clinic Supply

Scheduled restocking and delivery of medical consumables, gloves, syringes, and IV kits to healthcare providers.

Vaccination Distribution

Cold-chain compliant delivery of vaccines to hospitals, clinics, and mobile health units across regional hubs.
